# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment or discriminatory remarks
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information
- Any conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report incidents to project maintainers. Consequences may include warnings, removals, or bans from the project.
